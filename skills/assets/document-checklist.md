# NRI Document Checklist

## Purpose
This checklist helps NRI taxpayers and the advisory team collect the right documents before tax analysis and filing begin. Only documents relevant to the case need to be provided. Use alongside the NRI Client Intake Questionnaire.

## 1. Core Documents — Usually Required
- PAN card copy
- Aadhaar card (if available)
- Passport copy (first and last page + visa stamps for stay days)
- Passport travel pages or travel summary for the relevant FY
- Prior year ITR acknowledgements
- AIS copy
- Form 26AS copy
- TIS copy
- 26AS / AIS / TIS downloads (latest from portal)
- Bank statements or interest certificates relevant to Indian income
- NRO / NRE bank statements
- Form 16 (if salaried NRI with Indian employer)
- Form 16A (TDS on interest, rent, professional fees)
- Form 16B from property buyer (Section 194IA / 195 TDS)
- TDS certificates (if applicable)
- Advance tax challan receipts (Challan 280)
- TCS certificates (for LRS remittances)
- Any income tax notices received

## 2. Residency / Stay Verification
- Passport travel pages showing arrival and departure stamps
- Travel summary or stay-day computation sheet
- Visa / residence permit copy (if relevant)
- Tax Residency Certificate (TRC) from foreign country
- Tax residency certificate or equivalent foreign residency evidence (if available)
- Brief note on family / home / business presence in India (if material)

## 3. Indian Income Documents

### A. Salary / Employment-Linked
- Form 16
- Salary slips (if relevant)
- Employment letter or summary (if needed for context)
- ESOP / RSU / stock-linked papers (if applicable)

### B. House Property / Rental
- Rent agreement + rent receipts (if rental income)
- Rent summary for the year
- Tenant details (if available)
- Lease / rent agreement (if available)
- Municipal tax or related expense support (if relevant)
- Loan interest certificate (if relevant)

### C. Interest / Dividend / Passive
- Bank interest certificates
- FD certificates and interest computation
- FD interest certificate
- NRO account statement
- Dividend statement or broker statement
- Any other passive income summary

### D. Business / Professional
- Invoice summary or receipt summary
- Bank statement reflecting receipts
- Basic P&L or earning summary (if available)
- GST summary (if relevant)

## 4. Capital Gains / Transaction Documents

### A. Property Sale / Transfer
- Sale deed + registration documents (if property sold)
- Purchase deed
- Stamp duty valuation certificate
- Cost / improvement documents (if any)
- Buyer TDS proof / Form 16B or related evidence (if available)
- Section 197 lower TDS certificate (if obtained)
- Working of sale consideration received (if needed)
- Joint ownership details (if relevant)
- Inheritance / gift background papers (if relevant)

### B. Listed Shares / Mutual Funds / Securities
- Demat / broker capital gains statement
- Broker statement
- Capital gains statement
- Contract notes (if needed)
- Holding statement / transaction statement
- Dividend statement (if relevant)

### C. Unlisted Shares / Startup Equity / Private Transfer
- Share transfer agreement or transfer papers
- Cap table / ownership proof (if available)
- Share certificate copies (if available)
- Valuation support (if available and relevant)
- Consideration and payment support

### D. ESOP / RSU / Stock-Linked Sale
- ESOP / RSU vesting and exercise statements
- Grant / vesting documents
- Exercise details (if applicable)
- Sale statement
- Employer-provided stock records (if available)
- Tax withholding details (if any)

## 5. Foreign / Cross-Border Documents
- Foreign payslips
- Employer tax summary
- Foreign tax certificate / withholding proof
- Foreign tax return / tax computation (for FTC)
- Foreign return copy (if available)
- Foreign bank or investment income statement (if relevant)
- Tax Residency Certificate (TRC) from foreign country
- Tax residency certificate or other residence proof (if available)
- Documents relating to foreign stock / equity transactions (if relevant)
- Form 15CA / 15CB (if previously filed for remittances)

## 6. Deduction & Investment Proofs
- PPF / NPS / ELSS investment proofs (for Section 80C)
- Health insurance premium receipts (for Section 80D)
- Education loan interest certificate (for Section 80E)
- Donation receipts with PAN of donee (for Section 80G)

## 7. Department / Notice History Documents
- Notice copies
- Defective return communications
- Scrutiny / reassessment notices
- Assessment order (if any)
- Prior response submitted to the department
- Intimation under processing (if relevant)

## 8. Optional but Helpful
- Self-prepared summary of income heads
- Self-prepared travel-day summary
- Tax computation from prior advisor (if available)
- Transaction summary sheet for property / securities sale
- Short note describing unusual receipts or transactions

## 9. Case-Based Quick Reference

**Simple bank interest only:** PAN, AIS, 26AS, TIS, bank interest certificate, prior ITR acknowledgement.

**Rent + interest:** All basic documents + rent summary, lease details, bank interest certificate, TDS support.

**Property sale:** All basic documents + sale deed, purchase deed, cost/improvement records, buyer TDS support, bank/receipt trail.

**Shares / MF / startup equity:** All basic documents + broker/transfer statement, capital gains statement, ownership/transfer support, cost basis details.

**Foreign tax credit / DTAA:** All basic documents + foreign payslips/tax summary, foreign tax certificate, tax residency evidence, foreign return copy.

## 10. Internal Use Notes
- Missing travel data may block residency lock.
- Missing transaction papers may block filing lock.
- Missing TDS support may affect reconciliation and final computation.
- Missing foreign tax evidence may block DTAA / FTC positioning.
- Files with major gaps should be routed as Amber or Red, not treated as routine filing.
